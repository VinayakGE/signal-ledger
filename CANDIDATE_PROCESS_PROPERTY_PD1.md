# Candidate Process Property — PD1

**Date:** 2026-06-23

**Status:** Emerging, Unvalidated, Behavioral

---

## What Was Observed

Across multiple investigations, the repository has repeatedly:

1. Made a claim
2. Identified claim/evidence mismatch
3. Demoted the claim

**That pattern exists.**

---

## What Was Inferred (Candidate Only)

The repository may possess a developing capacity for self-correction.

**Notice the modality:** may

Not: does

Not: has

Not: most durable survivor

---

## The Distinction From Objects

### PP#002 Can Be Attacked By Evidence
Example wound: "Here is contemporaneous documentation of motivation"

### PD1 Can Only Be Attacked By Behavior

**Wound example 1:** Future case produces over-claim + counter-evidence → Repository refuses demotion

**Wound example 2:** Future case produces favorite explanation → Repository lowers standards → No demotion

**What counts as wound:** Repository fails to demote a claim when mismatch is large

---

## Why This Is Different Category

**Objects:** Explanations, boundaries, instruments

**Process properties:** Behaviors measured over time

**The problem with small samples:** Process properties cannot be validated from limited observation

**Current sample size:** 5 instances of demotion

**Sufficient to establish pattern?** Maybe

**Sufficient to establish robustness?** No

---

## What Would Be Needed For Validation

### Scenario A: Weak Test (Already Observed)
- Unpopular claim is made
- Counter-evidence appears
- Repository demotes claim
- No emotional attachment to defend

**Status:** Observed multiple times

**What it proves:** Repository can demote when dispassionate

**What it doesn't prove:** Repository can demote when emotionally invested

---

### Scenario B: Strong Test (Not Yet Observed)
- Repository's favorite explanation emerges
- Repository emotionally invested
- Counter-evidence appears
- Repository still demotes

**Status:** Not yet tested

**What it would prove:** Demotion works even under pressure

**What it would mean:** PD1 is actually robust

---

## The Real Test of Promotion Discipline

Not: Can it demote weak claims?

**But:** Can it demote claims the repository wants to keep?

Until the repository demonstrates that, PD1 remains candidate, not confirmed.
