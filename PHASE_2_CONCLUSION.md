**Verdict: SURVIVING**

**Status post-WeWork:**

### Object 3: PP#002 (Observable Selection, Opaque Motivation)

**Pre-registered prediction:** "Organizational selections are observable; motivations remain unrecoverable"

**Verdict: SURVIVING**

**Status post-WeWork:**
- Confidence: High (independent appearance; no retrofitting)
- Domain applicability: Universal (appears in founder-led AND institutional)
- Strength: High (confirmed across 6 cases)
- Survival: High (survived adversarial test)
- Generativity: Medium (guides investigation; unclear predictive power)

**Promotion level:** PP#002 advances from "Survived compression" to "Validated through adversarial test"
